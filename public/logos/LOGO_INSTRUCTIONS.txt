================================================================================
LOGO SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
Wits Cyber Security Registration Portal
================================================================================

REQUIRED LOGOS:
---------------

1. wits-logo.png
   - Official University of the Witwatersrand logo
   - Recommended: 120x120 pixels (transparent background)
   - Where to get: Wits branding resources or marketing department
   
2. openvpn-logo.png
   - OpenVPN official logo
   - Recommended: 90x90 pixels (transparent background)
   - Where to get: https://openvpn.net/press-kit/ or similar

INSTALLATION:
-------------
1. Download or obtain the logos
2. Rename them exactly as:
   - wits-logo.png
   - openvpn-logo.png
3. Place them in this folder: public/logos/

CURRENT STATUS:
---------------
The application is configured to display these logos but will gracefully
handle missing images. When you add the logo files, they will appear
automatically in the header without any code changes needed.

DESIGN NOTES:
-------------
- The page uses Wits colors: Navy Blue (#003366) and Gold (#FFD700)
- White background with gradient blue header
- Logos are positioned in the top header section
- Modern, clean card-based design throughout
